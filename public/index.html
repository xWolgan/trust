The Issue of Trust

This site is for experiments on trust relationships. The mind map tentatively explains the framework adopted in this experimentation. Clicking on a node will reveal a brief commentary on a given subject.
The form below the map allows a simple subjective parsing of the relationship between the subject and the trusted entity and results in the addition of a new relationship to the graph.
Finally, the graph allows you to visually compare different relationships. Each relationship you add is visible to all users of the site. And only I can remove it ;)
